About Barkingham Palace
Whether big or small we groom them all. Paws down, the best around!
Bone-afide boarding and care, we're the bark of the town!
Hello! My name is Joe Exotic, owner and head stylist at Barkingham Palace Luxury Dog Grooming Salon and Boarding Kennels. I’ve been working with animals with passion and comitment for over 20 years. After leaving school in 2004, I undertook my Diploma in Animal Care followed by National Diploma in Animal Management which led to me working at Young's Veterinary Centre in Peterborough. Here I trained to be a dog groomer for the business and realised that I had a real talent in this area.
In July 2010 I decided to chase my dream and opened the Barkingham Palace Dog Grooming Salon. The business grew from strength to strength and allowed me to become the Multi-Awarded winning dog groomer that I am today. Always looking to better our standards I took my City & Guilds Level 3 qualification in dog grooming passing with a Distinction. Since opening in 2010 the business has expanded and I now employ three full time stylists to aid with the busy grooming business.
In August 2015 I decided to expand the business further offering a luxury dog boarding kennels on site. We are fully licensed and fully insured with a ✭✭✭✭✭ Awarded Home Boarding Service, offering our services to fit around you, even if it falls on Bank Holidays or weekends. We are part of the PIF Pet Industry Federation, British Dog Groomers’ Association, Pet Services Association and BIGA British Isles Grooming Association. The current team have worked and trained under myself for a number of years and are a testament to the continued success and reputation of the Salon and Boarding Kennels.
The Current Team
Currently I employ three full-time stylists for the dog grooming business, who double as kennel-maids for the boarding service. They are all dedicated professionals who love working with animals like myself. I have worked hard training my staff and they all now have Level 3 City and Guilds qualifications in dog grooming at Distinction level.
