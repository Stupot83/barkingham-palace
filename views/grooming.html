Luxury Grooming Services at Barkingham Palace Salon
Whether big or small we groom them all. Paws down, the best around!
We provide a wide range of services covering all your dog grooming needs under one roof. Whether it’s your puppy’s first groom or your dog’s regular “clip & trim”, our bespoke service is based on breed characteristics, your preferences and your pet’s own nature and health, we can provide the perfect package at competitive and affordable rates. All our canine grooming equipment and tools are professional-salon standard and of course we are a multi-award winning professional dog groomers as well as life-long dog lovers.
Full Groom for Long Haired Breeds - From £27 per dog
Our Full Groom service for Long Haired Breeds includes a pre-groom, bath, brush, blow dry, full body styling, eyes and ears cleaned and nail clip. Prices will vary depending on size of dog and coat condition. For a personalised quote for your dog, please contact us.
Full Groom for Smooth Haired Breeds – From £27 per dog
Our Full Groom service for Smooth Haired Breeds has been developed especially for short/smooth coated dog breeds that may not need a full styling. Each of these grooms includes a pre-groom, bath, brush, blow dry, eyes and ears cleaned and nail clip.
Bath, Brush & Blow Dry - From £20 per dog
Perfect for in-between groom, to keep your dog looking their best.
Puppy Groom - Only £20 per puppy
Perfect for introducing your puppy to grooming, this dedicated puppy groom is suitable for puppies from three to six months old. The groom includes a bath, brush through of the coat, gentle blow dry, eyes and ears cleaned, paw tidy if needed and nail clip.
The deShedding Treatment - £8 extra
Perfect for dog breeds that are prone to shedding. Our deShedding service includes: Pre bath deShedding with FURminator tool selected specifically for your pet Bath with FURminator deShedding shampoo and conditioner Brush and blow dry Additional final deShedding for a perfect finish Not suitable for all dog coats. Please speak with the team for more information.
The VIP Treatment - £10 extra
Our gold standard of spa treatment, this luxury service, which can be added to your dog’s groom for only £10 includes: Application of teeth clean gel and fresh breath foam, Paw wax, SPA™ blueberry facial, Luxury or Natural shampoo, Hydrating butter treatment and Spritz of fragrance.
The Poodles & Oodles Treatment - £8 extra
A dedicated spa treatment with the sweet scent of chocolate that is tailored to support the unique needs of wool coated dogs, including: Choice of Stand Proud or 2 in 1 conditioning shampoo, Comb through spray to tease out tangles, Application of teeth clean gel and fresh breath foam, Spritz of fragrance.
Pick & Mix Treatments – Any 3 for £5
If you don't need a whole package, why not consider our Pick & Mix treatments? Select three treatments from the menu below for only £5 extra. Luxury shampoo, Natural Shampoo, Deep coat re-moisturising conditioner, Application of teeth clean gel, Application of fresh breath foam, Paw tidy and wax, Tear stain remover, Ear clean, Spritz of fragrance.
Little Paws Puppy Treatment - £10 extra
The ideal treatment for the new arrivals in your home, this puppy spa treatment is perfect for puppies aged between 3-6 months to make their first puppy groom that extra bit special. Our Little Paws service includes: Luxury shampoo, Blueberry facial, Hydrating butter treatment, Paw and nose treatment, Application of puppy teeth clean gel.
