Luxury Boarding Services at Barkingham Palace Kennels
Bone-afide boarding and care, we're the bark of the town!
Are you faced with puppy dog eyes and a rather disgruntled looking pooch whenever you fish out the suitcases for your holidays? Or are you in need of care for your beloved pet while in hospital? Or maybe you fancy a night-out or a long weekend away with your friends and/or family?
We all too familiar with the guilt of jetting off to sunnier climates while our “fur-babies” are left behind. And worrying about the well-being of your pet when you are under the weather is the last thing you need. That’s why we are pleased to offer Luxury Home Boarding for your beloved pets!
We provide a wide range of services covering all your dog boarding needs under one roof. We are a FULLY LICENSED and FULLY INSURED ✭✭✭✭ Awarded Home Boarding Service, and as our grooming services are bespoke, so is our Luxury Home Boarding, so we can offer our services to fit around you, even if it falls on Bank Holidays.

Grooming Customers
If you are a customer for our Grooming Services already, you can simply call us, message us, or just come to the Salon and we can book dates for your fur-baby to stay with us. If you are a new customer for boarding once you have agreed a stay with us we can also offer a range of bespoke grooming services for your dog(s) which can be provided during their stay with us.
New Customers
If you are new to us, please give us a quick message with dogs breed, sex and dates that you would like to book for. After you have done that, we can arrange to meet you and your dog(s) for a FREE consultation “Meet & Greet”, at the same time we can learn about your dog’s routine, food, any medical requirements, favourite toys etc. We often do a trial stay for a day to ensure that you, your dog and we are happy.
Booking Process
We will email you a booking form for each dog, which has to be filled, signed online and emailed back, as well as 50% deposit which has to be paid within 7 days of receiving form to secure dates that you would like, otherwise they can be offered to someone else.
Booking Terms
50% deposit is due at the time of booking. Your booking can only be confirmed when both the deposit and the signed booking form have been received. At Barkingham Palace we are not able to keep home boarding reservations open for more than 7 days so bookings will be released.
